Abort Respect India

Respect is a pioneering nongovernmental organisation (NGO) with a base in India that focuses on the continuation of governance (COG) and the sustainability of community progress (SCOP) using technology. "Smart Community" is something we advocate.

Using the idea of "Smart Government" to improve growth possibilities for the most vulnerable members of society in a safer and more secure setting. The goal of RESPECT is to investigate how cutting-edge technological tools may most effectively serve society as a whole, without any form of bias.
